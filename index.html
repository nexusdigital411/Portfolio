// My Story

Adversity Didn't Stop Me.
It Taught Me.

Most people assume you need two working hands to fix things. I'm proof that assumption is wrong. After losing full use of my hands, I had a choice: give up the work I loved, or find a new way to do it.

I chose to adapt. I spent years developing techniques, tools, and methods that let me diagnose, repair, and solve problems that stump conventional technicians. The limitation forced me to become more methodical, more patient, and more creative than I ever was before.

What you get when you hire me isn't just a repair. You get someone who has fought for every bit of knowledge they have — who doesn't take shortcuts because the work itself is what matters.

If it's broken, I want to know why. And then I want to fix it.

CIS BENCHMARKS L1 / L2

Hardening scripts validated against CIS Level 1 (essential hygiene) and Level 2 (high-security environments). Applied to Linux, macOS, and network devices with scored audit output.

NIST SP 800-53

NIST Security Controls

Control family mapping across AC, AU, CM, IA, SI and more. Automated assessment scripts produce NIST-aligned findings reports suitable for federal and enterprise environments.

CMMC 2.0

Practice-level evidence collection and gap analysis for CMMC Levels 1–3. Supports DoD contractor readiness with documented POA&M output and system security plan templates.

Full-lifecycle pen testing following PTES phases: pre-engagement, intelligence gathering, threat modeling, exploitation, post-exploitation, and reporting. Legal authorization required and verified.
